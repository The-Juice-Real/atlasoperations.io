项目提案：分布式创作者零售网络

To: Lumina & Partners  |  Date: December 2, 2025
1. Executive Summary 项目摘要

We are deploying a "Business-in-a-Box" model. Instead of paying influencers for posts, we build them white-labeled stores.

我们正在部署“一站式商业”模式。我们不再按帖子付费给网红，而是为他们建立白牌商店。
  • The Asset (Business-in-a-Box): We give creators a branded storefront (e.g. "Sarah's Wellness Shop"). You provide the supply chain. We provide the backend operations. 资产（一站式商业）：我们为创作者提供品牌店面（例如“Sarah的健康店”）。您提供供应链。我们提供后台运营。
  • The Enablement: We use the Atlas AI Training Protocol (repackaged from Creator Cash Sprint) to train partners on sales & compliance automatically. 赋能：我们使用 Atlas AI 培训协议（重新打包自 Creator Cash Sprint）自动培训合伙人销售技巧和合规性。
2. Operational Mechanics 运营机制
A. White-Label Technology (白牌技术)

We clone the "Master Store" infrastructure for each partner. To the customer, it looks like the Creator's own brand. To us, it is a centralized order stream.

我们为每个合伙人克隆“主店”基础设施。对客户来说，它是创作者的自有品牌。对我们来说，它是集中的订单流。
B. The "Operator" Model (运营者模式)

Atlas is the Invisible Operator. We handle store updates, customer service coordination, and partner training. The Creator focuses 100% on Content. Lumina focuses 100% on Logistics.

Atlas 是隐形运营者。 我们处理店铺更新、客服协调和合伙人培训。创作者 100% 专注于内容。Lumina 100% 专注于物流。
